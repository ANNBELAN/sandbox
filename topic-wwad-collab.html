Why it matters

We can support Canadians in making healthy food choices, if we develop and implement policies based on solid research and evidence.

Who is involved

Health Canada is committed to making this country's population among the healthiest in the world

Health Canada is committed to improving the lives of all of Canada's people and to making this country's population among the healthiest in the world as measured by longevity, lifestyle and effective use of the public health care system.
